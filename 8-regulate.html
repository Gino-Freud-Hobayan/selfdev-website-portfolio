Deliverable 3 - Trigger Tracker Poster

1. Trigger: Failure

Adopt a growth mindset
"Why am I being punished" to "What actions can I do now"
Don't make the same mistake twice, learn from it, and do better next time.

2. Trigger: Being compared to others

Stay on your own lane and focus on your own progress.
"What ultimately matters in this course is not so much where you end up relative to your classmates but where you end up relative to yourself when you began"

3. Trigger: Betrayal

Focus on what you can control and you have to be your own superman and save yourself.
Don't let a single bad apple spoil the whole batch, find a way to trust other people again
because not everybody is out to get you.
