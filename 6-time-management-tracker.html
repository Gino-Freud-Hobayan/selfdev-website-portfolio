Stress and Conflict Management:

Reflection:

Tracking my activities for an entire week made me more aware on how I actually spend my 24 hours a day.
How much time I spend being productive, engaging in escapism, and how much time I spend on other activities.
And that's a good thing because awareness is the first step in the problem solving process.
You cannot solve if you're not aware that a problem exists.
