"The same boiling water that softens the potato hardens the egg.
It's about what you're made of, not the circumstances."
- Unknown



I really like this quote as it perfectly represents what growth mindset is,
and because I am both the egg and the potato.

I'm not perfect and I'm not where I want to be yet, but I know that I can always grow and improve.

Sometimes I'm the egg, because what doesn't kill me only makes me stronger.
Sometimes I'm the potato, some challenges are just too big for me to handle and I crumble.

When life puts me in the boiling water, I always strive to do my best to adapt and harden like the egg.

In order to be like the egg, I only focus on what I can control, and that is the present.
I cannot change the past and the future is built one day at a time.
