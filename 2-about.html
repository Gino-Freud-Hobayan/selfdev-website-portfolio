About Me

It was hard since I was quiet, introverted, and didn't really have a big network. Working in that industry was hard but not impossible, so I worked hard and I was able to develop my skills, maintained a growth mindset, and through my efforts and persistence, I was able to survive in this challenging environment.

While working in sales I spent my free time learning SQL, Python, and Microsoft Excel.
I made my own portfolio and I eventually got a short internship as a data analyst but realized that I still lack a lot of skills and knowledge in this field, and that led me here to CIIT.
